Good to Know

The details.

How is the music chosen?

Every event is custom. We build it around your taste, your must-plays, and your no-gos — then I read the room and mix it live so the night flows.

What does it cost?

Pricing is tailored to your event — date, hours, and the details. Reach out and I'll put together a quote.

Who's the DJ on the day?

Me. The DJ you book is the DJ who shows up — no swapping in someone you've never met.

What about sound?

I bring professional, full-range live sound plus wireless mics for speeches and announcements, dialed in to your venue and guest count — with backup gear always on hand.

Where do you work?

Based in Overland Park and serving the Greater Kansas City metro. Traveling a little further? Just ask.

What about lighting?

Most KC weddings are at venues that handle their own lighting and layout. We'll talk through your setup so the music fits right in.
